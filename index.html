About

I'm a natural problem-solver with a love of learning and an entrepreneurial spirit, and I get most excited when I have the opportunity to wear multiple hats and work where tech, art, and thought intersect.

Currently, I'm Managing Partner at Quality x Quantity, a Houston-based AV company, where we provide awesome live sound, lighting, and staging to local entertainers and event planners.

Before QxQ, I spent 6 years in the military learning leadership skills and becoming highly skilled at troubleshooting while working as in IT.

As a developer, I'm preoccupied with learning about and participating in the entire software development process while taking on challenges across the full stack, whether it involves front-end, back-end, or infrastructure work. I realized while immersed in code in a course that not only do I have an aptitude for programming, but I also have an appetite to learn both the art and science of it.

Because of my experience I can understand difficult subjects quickly, and communicate effectively about business and technical topics to both engineers and people with a background that is nontechnical.
