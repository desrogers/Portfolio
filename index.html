About Me

Full Stack Web Developer and U.S Navy veteran with 6 years experience as a system administrator and an equivalent amount as a small business owner and consultant.

I’ve always been interested in programming, but I never really had the time to learn enough to know whether a career as a developer was for me. Until I did, when I completed the Full Stack Flex Coding Bootcamp at The University of Texas at Austin. There I realized, while becoming pretty good with Javascript and the other parts of the MERN stack, that I’d love to continue learning, making things and solving problems, even if it meant I could only do it on my off time.

So I'm excited about developing expertise across the entire web development stack, and also learning some DevOps skills to leverage my experience as a System Administrator so I can contribute to exciting and innovative projects.

I think the skills I have and the roles I’ve had proves that I’m self-motivated, I’m great at solving problems, I can understand difficult subjects quickly, I can communicate well with people on both the business and technical side of things, and I can lead as well as follow.
